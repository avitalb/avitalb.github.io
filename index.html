i previously worked in progressive politics in a software and data engineering capacity, most recently at actblue.

my technical expertise lies in backend software engineering and data engineering. i feel particularly enthused about working with geographic and civic data, building elegant data pipelines, and implementing robust engineering, testing, and documentation processes. i also have a background in technology policy with a focus on privacy and security through the lens of protecting individual rights, as well as interest and background in energy, climate, and transportation policy.

i am passionate about using technology to fuel positive change (or at least, create useful tools to accomplish non-icky goals). i am excited about engineering and policy opportunities in mission-driven organizations in the private, nonprofit, and public sectors.
